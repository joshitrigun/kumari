## Practical Implementation Tips

### For Blender Grease Pencil:
1. **Set up color swatches** in the material properties with these HEX values
2. **Create lighting rigs** for each act:
   - Act 1: 3-point warm lighting (key, fill, rim)
   - Act 2: Single harsh side light (cool), transition rig
   - Act 3: One warm lamp + ambient dark
   - Act 4: Single warm sun + sky fill

3. **Use gradients** for transitions (especially 1:13 shift):
   - Keyframe color temperature in compositor
   - Animate light color & intensity

### For After Effects:
1. Create adjustment layers for each act with:
   - **Curves:** lift shadows (Act 1), crush shadows (Act 2)
   - **Color Balance:** warm (Act 1), cool (Act 2–3), warm-soft (Act 4)
   - **Levels:** adjust contrast per act

2. Use **Lumetri Color Grading** presets as starting points

3. Transition LUTs at 1:13 (sudden) and 2:40 (gradual fade)

### For DaVinci Resolve:
1. Create node-based color correction per shot
2. Use **Fusion** for particle effects (incense, mist, light trails)
3. Grade with a LUT for Act 1 (golden), then adjust node-by-node for Acts 2–4
