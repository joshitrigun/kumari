Use this at the start of every prompt:

`Hyper-realistic ethnographic historical portrait, 18th-century Kathmandu Valley, Nepal. Grounded Nepali Newar ethnic facial features (blend of Tibeto-Burman and Indo-Aryan, high cheekbones, dark almond eyes). Authentic traditional Newar textiles: raw cotton, makhamali (velvet), and dhaka weave. Dark Sal wood carvings (ankhijhyal) and exposed Ma-Appa red brick. Lit by mustard oil brass lamps. A 50-year-old Nepali Newar man with medium brown skin, tired hollowed cheeks, firm jaw, slight mustache and stubble beard. He wears a traditional Malla-style tight-fitting upper garment (tapalan) of deep madder-dyed cotton, wrapped at the waist with a thick wound cloth (patuka), and a pleated lower skirt-wrap (jama). On his head is a traditional Malla multi-tiered gold repoussé headpiece, intricately hammered, sitting low on dark brushed-back hair. No large turbans. He has a red vermillion tika. ## Approval Rule

Reject any result that looks like:

- Malaysian songket or tengkolok/songkok.
- Thai or Balinese ceremonial silhouette.
- Mughal/Rajput fantasy palace character.
- Generic anime doll or Disney/princess design.
- Flat vector/SVG/cartoon mascot.
- Glamour goddess or romantic Taleju.
- Cute or spooky Kumari.

Approve only if a Nepali/Newar viewer can immediately feel Kathmandu Valley / Newar / Malla influence.
