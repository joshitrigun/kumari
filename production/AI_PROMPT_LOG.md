# The King's Regret — Master AI Prompt Log
This log contains the definitive production prompts for use in **Veo** and **Seedance 2.0**. Use these to maintain visual and historical consistency across the film.

---

## 🎨 MASTER STYLE CONSISTENCY (Visual DNA)
*Add this block to the beginning or end of every prompt.*

**Style Block:**
> "Style: Cinematic 2.5D stylized animation. Aesthetic: Hand-drawn digital paint with visible fine brush-texture. Depth: Shallow depth of field with soft-focus backgrounds. Detail Level: High-fidelity skin with sub-surface scattering, detailed Newar architectural carvings. Motion: Fluid cinematic 24fps, purposeful and slow character movement."

**Negative Prompt:**
> "Disney style, 3D CGI, plastic skin, neon colors, fast jerky motion, futuristic technology, Western features, messy lineart, superhero mask makeup, oversaturated primary colors, modern clothing, bright daylight."

## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT PRODUCTION PROMPTS

### SHOT 1: THE ESTABLISH (Malla-Authentic)
**Goal:** Lock the environment style and historical accuracy.
**Prompt:**
> "Wide cinematic interior of a 17th-century Malla King's inner chamber in Kathmandu. Walls of rich red Ma-Appa bricks and thick carved Sal-wood pillars. Background features a large 'Ankhijhyal' (peacock-lattice window) with soft twilight. Red mud-plastered floor with heavy Sukul mats. Center: very low carved wooden 'Chokhat' board on a royal velvet Gaddi cushion. Flickering brass oil diyas in wall niches, deep amber light, volumetric incense smoke. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 2: THE APPROACH (Camera Motion)
**Goal:** Establish the slow, reverent dolly motion toward the sacred game.
**Prompt:**
> "Cinematic slow dolly-in shot toward a low carved wooden game table (Chokhat) in a candlelit Malla chamber. The camera moves gracefully over heavy Sukul mats. In the background, the soft silhouettes of the King and the Goddess are visible. The focus shifts from the room's architecture to the intricate gold-inlay patterns on the table surface. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 3: THE SACRED GAME (Detail/Prop Focus)
**Goal:** High-detail focus on the game pieces and the 'Token'.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up of regal hands moving a carved gold lotus token across a dark Sal-wood board. The token catches the warm flicker of nearby oil lamps, casting dancing shadows. The skin of the hands shows high-fidelity texture and warm sub-surface scattering. Other game pieces made of ivory and semi-precious stones are scattered across the board in a complex arrangement. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 4: THE WITNESS & THE GAZE (Character Intro)
**Goal:** Introduce the King and Goddess's presence.
**Prompt:**
> "Cinematic medium shot of the Malla King and Goddess Taleju sitting across from the low game board. The King looks at the Goddess with profound reverence. Cut-away/Insert: A Newar Queen is seen briefly peeking through an ornate dark-wood 'Ankhijhyal' (lattice window) in the background shadows, her gold 'Bulaki' (nose-ring) glinting. The atmosphere is thick with incense and spiritual weight. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 5: THE INTIMACY PEAK (Character Identity Hit)
**Goal:** Lock the King and Taleju's faces. This is the highest consistency risk.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up alternating between a regal Malla King (middle-aged, strong jaw, slight beard) and the ethereal Goddess Taleju (golden skin, large kohl-rimmed eyes). Their eyes meet in a moment of deep spiritual connection. The King's pupils subtly dilate. Caustic, liquid light patterns ripple across the King's face, reflecting the Goddess's divine presence. Ethereal golden aura surrounds Taleju. High-fidelity skin with warm sub-surface scattering. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 6: THE FLASH OF DESIRE (The Pivot)
**Goal:** Capture the live lighting shift and character acting shift.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up of the King's face as the light suddenly shifts from warm gold to harsh, cold slate-blue. His expression hardens from devotion to possessive greed; his eye glint sharpens to a metallic edge. A single strand of hair falls loose from his tiered gold crown. His hand on the carved board visibly clenches, knuckles whitening. [STYLE BLOCK] Lighting: Live transition from 2700K warm gold to 8000K cold slate-blue, harsh side-light, high-contrast black shadows."

### SHOT 7: THE TRANSGRESSION (The Reach)
**Goal:** Dramatic action of the King breaking the sacred distance.
**Prompt:**
> "Medium close-up as the King reaches across the table, his hand entering the Goddess's personal space. The Goddess Taleju recoils in visible wounded shock, her ethereal glow flickering like a dying candle. The harsh slate-blue side-lighting emphasizes the sharp angles of the King's face. Geometric shadows from the lattice window stretch across the floor. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 2 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 8: THE VANISHING (Emotional Climax)
**Goal:** Render the upward dissolution and the physics of the falling token.
**Prompt:**
> "The Goddess Taleju dissolves from the feet up into shimmering golden particles that rise upward like smoke into the dark rafters. As her hands vanish, a heavy carved lotus token falls from her translucent grip and hits the wooden table with a physical thud, rolling slightly into shadow. The King sits frozen in horror, his hands cupping the empty space. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 2 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 9: THE MEMORY (Flashback)
**Goal:** A brief, hauntingly beautiful contrast to the current coldness.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up of the Goddess Taleju's face, smiling with genuine divine compassion. The lighting is ultra-warm and soft. This is a flickering memory, slightly blurred at the edges with a dream-like bloom. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 10: THE RAIN (Transition to Act 3)
**Goal:** Establish the change in setting and the King's broken state.
**Prompt:**
> "Wide exterior shot of a narrow Kathmandu alleyway during a heavy monsoon downpour at night. The Malla King, now without his crown or royal coat, walks alone in a simple damp grey cotton wrap. He is hunched, defeated. Monochromatic slate-grey and deep blue tones dominate, except for a single distant flickering lantern. The red bricks are slick and reflective. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 3 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 11: THE DREAM (Spiritual Vision)
**Goal:** Create an ethereal, low-contrast encounter.
**Prompt:**
> "Cinematic medium shot in a thick, monochromatic grey mist. The Goddess Taleju appears as a soft-focus, translucent silhouette. She is not glowing as before, but seems made of the mist itself. She leans forward, her lips moving in silent, compassionate instruction. The atmosphere is quiet and heavy. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 3 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 12: THE PENITENCE (Courtyard Meditation)
**Goal:** Focus on the King's transformation through suffering.
**Prompt:**
> "Medium shot of the King meditating in a stone temple courtyard under heavy rain. He sits perfectly still as water streams down his pale, aged face. His posture is humble. Beside him, a single oil lamp is protected by a small stone niche, its flame fighting the wind. High-diffusion atmosphere. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 3 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 13: THE MONTAGE (The Long Wait)
**Goal:** Use symbolic objects to show the passage of time/grief.
**Prompt:**
> "A cinematic montage of symbolic objects: A row of burning candles melting down; the carved lotus token resting on a damp stone surface; incense sticks turning to ash; the King's weathered hands clasped in prayer. Everything is soaked in the desaturated blues and greys of the monsoon. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 3 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 14: THE AWAKENING (The Final Instruction)
**Goal:** The bridge between grief and hope.
**Prompt:**
> "Close-up of the King's eyes as they snap open. The monochromatic blue of the rain starts to be pierced by a faint, warm peach glow from the horizon. The sound of the rain softens. He looks toward the temple entrance with a sudden spark of realization. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 3→4 Transition]"

### SHOT 15: THE KUMARI (The Miracle)
**Goal:** Introduce the child with maximum visual impact and reverence.
**Prompt:**
> "Wide-angle low-shot of a small Newar girl (6-8 years old) walking slowly out of the temple shadows into the first light of dawn. She is dressed in vibrant red and gold ceremonial robes, her face painted with intricate kohl and a third eye. She carries the golden lotus token, which glows with a soft, liquid light. The sky behind her is a beautiful palette of peach and lavender. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 4 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 16: THE RECOGNITION (Collective Peace)
**Goal:** Show the community and the King's acceptance.
**Prompt:**
> "Cinematic medium shot of the King and a small crowd of devotees bowing low as the child passes. The King's face is bathed in the dawn light; he is crying quiet, humble tears of relief. The architecture of the courtyard is rim-lit by the rising sun, looking luminous and hopeful. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 4 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 17: THE EPILOGUE (The New Sacred)
**Goal:** Final symbolic closure.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up of the golden lotus token being placed onto the game board by the child's small hand. The light is perfectly clear and warm. The camera pans up to the King's face as he looks at the child and smiles—a simple, human smile of peace. Fade to white. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 4 LIGHTING]"
