## 🎬 SHOT-BY-SHOT PRODUCTION PROMPTS

### SHOT 1: THE ESTABLISH (Malla-Authentic)
**Goal:** Lock the environment style and historical accuracy.
**Prompt:**
> "Wide cinematic interior of a 17th-century Malla King's inner chamber in Kathmandu. Walls of rich red Ma-Appa bricks and thick carved Sal-wood pillars. Background features a large 'Ankhijhyal' (peacock-lattice window) with soft twilight. Red mud-plastered floor with heavy Sukul mats. Center: very low carved wooden 'Chokhat' board on a royal velvet Gaddi cushion. Flickering brass oil diyas in wall niches, deep amber light, volumetric incense smoke. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 4: THE QUEEN'S WITNESS (Malla-Authentic acting)
**Goal:** Introduce the Queen's reverent witness moment with historical jewelry focus.
**Prompt:**
> "Cinematic medium close-up of a Newar Queen peeking through an ornate dark-wood 'Ankhijhyal' (lattice window). Only her eyes and her traditional gold jewelry are sharply lit by flickering candlelight. She wears a large gold 'Bulaki' (nose-ring) and heavy 'Tuki' (ear-ornaments) that catch the warm glint. Her eyes are wide with awe and a hint of spiritual longing, a tear-line highlight visible on her lower lid. The heavy wooden lattice partially obscures her face, creating beautiful geometric shadows. In the background, the soft warm glow of the sacred chamber is visible through the screen. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 5: THE INTIMACY PEAK (Character Identity Hit)
**Goal:** Lock the King and Taleju's faces. This is the highest consistency risk.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up alternating between a regal Malla King (middle-aged, strong jaw, slight beard) and the ethereal Goddess Taleju (golden skin, large kohl-rimmed eyes). Their eyes meet in a moment of deep spiritual connection. The King's pupils subtly dilate. Caustic, liquid light patterns ripple across the King's face, reflecting the Goddess's divine presence. Ethereal golden aura surrounds Taleju. High-fidelity skin with warm sub-surface scattering. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 1 LIGHTING]"

### SHOT 6: THE FLASH OF DESIRE (The Pivot)
**Goal:** Capture the live lighting shift and character acting shift.
**Prompt:**
> "Extreme close-up of the King's face as the light suddenly shifts from warm gold to harsh, cold slate-blue. His expression hardens from devotion to possessive greed; his eye glint sharpens to a metallic edge. A single strand of hair falls loose from his tiered gold crown. His hand on the carved board visibly clenches, knuckles whitening. [STYLE BLOCK] Lighting: Live transition from 2700K warm gold to 8000K cold slate-blue, harsh side-light, high-contrast black shadows."

### SHOT 8: THE VANISHING (Emotional Climax)
**Goal:** Render the upward dissolution and the physics of the falling token.
**Prompt:**
> "The Goddess Taleju recoils in wounded shock, stepping back into deep blue shadows. She dissolves from the feet up into shimmering golden particles that rise upward like smoke. As her hands vanish, a heavy carved lotus token falls from her translucent grip and hits the wooden table with a physical thud, rolling slightly into shadow. [STYLE BLOCK] [ACT 2 LIGHTING]"
